WOWHEAD

WoWHead is an incredible resource and can provide you with valuable information on just about anything in the game. It will take some time to familarize yourself with it. To keep things simple we will focus on just a few of its features. We will start with helping you figure out what class you want to play. When you first arive to WoWHead's landing page you will scroll over the databse button towards the top left of your screen. Underneath the character group you will scroll over the classes tab and select any class that sounds interesting. Within this database you will find a summary of the class and its specializations along with guides to help you build your character.

Next we will review how to make your character the coolest in your realm! Once again we go back to the database button on the landing page. From there you can scroll over the transmog tab in the items group. At this point you can select your class and the different types of transmogs it has. As you dig further it will show you what the set looks like and you can click on the items to find out where to get them in game. In just a short while you can be an unholy Death Knight with a taste for destruction!
